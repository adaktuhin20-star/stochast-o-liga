"Discover the elusive treasure behind the correct door."

In this round each team will be provided with 6 clues where the preceding clue will lead to the door of the next one.

Mind that, several doors hide the same clue, so a team can make a random lucky guess too! (Though, no points will be awarded for a lucky guess.) But a lucky guess can lead you to the treasure in much fewer steps. E.g Suppose you are in second clue and your lucky guess leads you to the sixth clue, then you will just be one clue away from the treasure! (Since the six clues are not interconnected.)

Opening the wrong door results in loss of life and each team will have 3 lives for the entire journey.

For each correct door you open, 5 points will be awarded and upon unlocking the treasure door, 10 extra points will be awarded.

Each team will get the same set of clues and time limit for the entire journey is 15 minutes.
